Cookie Policy

At Burban, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies on our website and how you can control them.

1. What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device when you visit a website. It allows the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as your language, font size, and other display settings) for a certain period of time, so you don’t have to re-enter them every time you visit the site or navigate between pages.

2. How Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance your browsing experience: Cookies help us personalize your experience and provide content tailored to your preferences.

  • Analyze our traffic: We use cookies to collect information about how visitors interact with our site to improve its performance.

  • Serve personalized ads: Some cookies allow us to show you ads that match your interests and browsing history.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for you to navigate our site and use its features.

  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how users interact with our site by collecting information about visited pages and any errors encountered.

  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences (such as language) to enhance your experience during future visits.

  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver personalized ads based on your interests and browsing habits.

4. How to Control Cookies?

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: You can configure your browser to accept or reject cookies or to alert you when a cookie is sent. Since each browser is different, refer to your browser’s help menu to learn how to adjust your cookie preferences.

  • Delete Cookies: You can delete cookies already placed on your device through your browser settings.

5. Consent to the Use of Cookies

By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser’s cookie settings.

6. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Cookie Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the date of the last update at the top. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed of any updates.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Cookie Policy, please feel free to contact us at: burban.assistance@outlook.com
