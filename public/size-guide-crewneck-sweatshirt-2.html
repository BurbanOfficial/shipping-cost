Discover our size guide to choose the perfect fit for you.
If you have any doubts, feel free to refer to our chart below!

A - Length

Place the end of the measuring tape next to the collar at the top of the long-sleeve top (the highest point of the shoulder).
Pull the tape down to the bottom of the top.

B - Chest

Measure yourself at the widest part of your chest.
Keep the measuring tape horizontal.

C - Sleeve Length

Place one end of the tape at the top of the raglan sleeve, then pull the tape down until you reach the wrist.
